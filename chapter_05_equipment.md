# Chapter 5: Equipment & Economy

Survival on the Rim requires the right tools. A sharp knife, a warm parka, and a pocket full of silver can make the difference between life and death.

---

## Economy
The universal currency of the Rim is **Silver**.
*   **Starting Wealth**: Determined by your Scenario (Chapter 2).
*   **Trade**: You can trade with visiting caravans or orbital trade ships (if you have a Comms Console).

### Market Value (Average)
*   **Meal**: 20 Silver
*   **Medicine**: 50 Silver
*   **Component**: 30 Silver
*   **Weapon**: 200 - 1000 Silver
*   **Slave**: 1500 Silver

---

## Weapons
Weapons define your combat capabilities.
*   **Damage**: The base damage dealt on a Tier 2 hit.
*   **Range**: The optimal distance in squares.
*   **AP**: Action Point cost to attack.

### Melee Weapons
Uses **Vigor** for attacks.

| Weapon | Damage | AP | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fists** | 2 | 2 | Blunt. |
| **Knife** | 3 | 2 | Sharp. Can be thrown (Range 5). |
| **Club** | 4 | 2 | Blunt. +1 Damage vs Armor. |
| **Spear** | 5 | 2 | Sharp. Reach (Attack 2 squares away). |
| **Mace** | 5 | 2 | Blunt. +1 Damage vs Armor. |
| **Longsword** | 6 | 2 | Sharp. High lethality. |
| **Monosword** | 8 | 2 | Sharp. Ignores 2 points of Armor. |
| **Zeushammer** | 8 | 2 | Blunt. EMP effect on hit (Stuns mechs). |

### Grenades
Thrown weapons. Always hit the target square, but can scatter on a miss.
*   **Frag Grenade**: 6 Damage. 3x3 Area. 3 AP.
*   **EMP Grenade**: Stuns Mechanoids/Turrets for 1 Round. 3x3 Area. 3 AP.
*   **Molotov**: Sets 3x3 Area on fire. 3 AP.

## Armor
Armor reduces incoming damage.
> **Damage Taken = Incoming Damage - Armor Rating**

| Armor | Rating | Speed | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tribalwear** | 0 | - | Basic clothing. |
| **Parka** | 1 | - | Cold Insulation. |
| **Duster** | 1 | - | Heat Insulation. |
| **Flak Vest** | 2 | - | Chest protection only. |
| **Flak Jacket** | 2 | - | Full body protection. |
| **Marine Armor** | 4 | -1 | Power Armor. High protection. |
| **Cataphract Armor** | 5 | -2 | Heavy Power Armor. Very slow. |

## Items & Drugs

### Medicine
Used to tend wounds and perform surgery.
*   **Herbal Medicine**: Tier 1. Heals **1d4 HP**.
*   **Industrial Medicine**: Tier 2. Heals **1d8 HP**.
*   **Glitterworld Medicine**: Tier 3. Heals **1d12 HP** and cures one Disease immediately.

### Drugs
Chemical assistance for life on the Rim.
*   **Smokeleaf**: **+4 Mood**. **-2 Bane** on all checks. Reduces Pain.
*   **Wake-up**: Regain **4 AP** immediately. Risk of Heart Attack.
*   **Go-Juice**: **+1 Speed**. Ignore all Pain penalties. **+2 Edge** on Melee.
*   **Yayo**: **+8 Mood**. **+1 Edge** on all checks. Highly Addictive.
*   **Luciferium**: **+2 to All Stats**. Heals old Scars. **Fatal Withdrawal**: If not taken every 6 days, you die in agony.
