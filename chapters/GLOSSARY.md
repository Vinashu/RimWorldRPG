# Glossary

Canonical terms used throughout the rulebook.

* **Action Points (AP)**: The 4-point action budget used each combat turn.
* **Attribute**: One of Vigor, Dexterity, Mind, or Spirit.
* **Augmented**: Major advantage; roll **3d10 (keep highest 2)**.
* **Bane**: A minor disadvantage, usually **-2**.
* **Base Die**: The non-story d10 in a Skill Check.
* **Colonist**: A player character.
* **Colonist Roll**: See **Skill Check**.
* **Complication**: Negative Story Die effect when the Story Die shows **10**.
* **Crafting Points (CP)**: Work cost for crafting and construction.
* **Defense Points (DP)**: Points generated by defenses for raid reactions.
* **Edge**: A minor advantage, usually **+2**.
* **Impaired**: Major disadvantage; roll **3d10 (keep lowest 2)**.
* **Inspiration Point**: A point spent to reroll, add a die, or reduce Stress.
* **Lifepath**: Character creation using Childhoods and Careers.
* **Mental Break**: A consequence of high Stress resolved by a Panic Check.
* **Mental Break Threshold**: The Stress value at which a Panic Check is triggered.
* **Need**: Core survival requirement (Food, Rest, Recreation, Beauty, Comfort).
* **Panic Check**: `1d6 + Current Stress`, used to resolve Mental Breaks.
* **Quadrum**: One of four 15-day seasons.
* **Range Band**: Melee, Close, Medium, or Far.
* **Recruit**: A non-player colonist added via colony events.
* **Research Points (RP)**: Progress points used to unlock technology.
* **RimWorld**: The video game that inspired this TTRPG.
* **Rimworld**: The frontier planet setting where the game takes place.
* **RimWorld RPG**: This tabletop roleplaying game.
* **Skill Check**: `Base Die + Story Die + Attribute + Skill Modifier`.
* **Skill Modifier**: `Floor(Skill Points / 10)`.
* **Skill Points**: Raw points in a skill (0â€“30).
* **Story Die**: The red d10 that creates Twists or Complications.
* **Storyteller (ST)**: The GM role that runs the world and adversaries.
* **Struggle**: Tier 1 success with a major cost.
* **Stress**: Accumulated pressure from needs and events.
* **Tier of Success**: The result band (Struggle, Success, Triumph).
* **Trait**: A defining advantage or drawback.
* **Triumph**: Tier 3 critical success.
* **Twist of Fate**: Positive Story Die effect when the Story Die shows **1**.
* **Wealth Score**: Colony total used for raid scaling and recruit limits.
