# Chapter 7: The Rim (World & Factions)

The Rim is a dangerous place, populated by diverse groups struggling for dominance.

---

## Factions
Your colony is not alone. You will interact with various factions, ranging from friendly traders to bloodthirsty pirates.

### Goodwill
Relationships are measured by **Goodwill**, ranging from **-100 to +100**.

| Status | Goodwill | Effects |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ally** | +75 to +100 | Will send military aid. Better trade prices (**-10% Buy / +10% Sell**). |
| **Neutral** | -9 to +74 | Will trade. Will not attack unprovoked. |
| **Hostile** | -100 to -10 | Will attack on sight. Cannot trade. |

### Major Factions
1.  **Civil Outlander Union** (Tech: **Industrial 2**): Survivors trying to rebuild. Friendly. Sources of Components/Medicine.
2.  **Rough Pig Union** (Tech: **Industrial 2**): Tough, xenophobic pig-people. Interactions are tense.
3.  **Savage Tribe** (Tech: **Neolithic 2**): Aggressive warriors. Low tech, high numbers. Source of Pemmican/Jade.
4.  **The Shattered Empire** (Tech: **Spacer 3**): Honor-bound remnants of a stellar empire. High-tech trade (Title required).
5.  **Pirate Gangs** (Tech: **Industrial 3**): **Always Hostile**. Raiders who live to plunder.

### Social Interactions
Your **Social** skill determines how you interact with the world.

*   **Trading**: **+2%** price improvement per Social Level (Max Â±40%).
*   **Recruitment**: Break down a prisoner's Will, then test Social to recruit.
*   **Peace Talks**: Attend talks to boost Goodwill (+50) or ruin it further (-20).

## Biomes

The environment is often your deadliest enemy.

### Temperate Forest
*   **Description**: lush forests, moderate temperatures, rich soil.
*   **Mechanics**:
    *   **Baseline**: No special penalties.
    *   **Disease**: Standard frequency.

### Arid Shrubland & Desert
*   **Description**: Hot, dry, endless sand and scrub.
*   **Mechanics**:
    *   **Heat**: Colonists without Dusters/Cowboy Hats gain **+1 Stress/Scene** during the day.
    *   **Scarce Food**: **Bane** on Foraging checks. **-2 Farming** (Base Modifier).
    *   **Visibility**: **+1 Base Defense**.

### Boreal Forest & Tundra
*   **Description**: Frozen pines, snow, and bitter cold.
*   **Mechanics**:
    *   **Cold**: Colonists without Parkas take **1d4 Damage** every hour.
    *   **Snow**: Deep snow counts as **Difficult Terrain** (Move costs +1 AP).
    *   **Winter**: **-1 Farming** (Base Modifier). **+1 Base Security**.

### Tropical Rainforest
*   **Description**: Choking vegetation, humid heat, and insects.
*   **Mechanics**:
    *   **Disease**: **Bane** on Vigor checks against disease.
    *   **Dense**: Movement is slow (Difficult Terrain default). **-1 Base Defense**.
    *   **Rich Soil**: **+1 Farming** (Base Modifier).
