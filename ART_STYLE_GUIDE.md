### Core Vibe

* Sci-fi frontier survival
* Hopeful but harsh
* Cozy-dangerous (RimWorld tone)
* Stylized, not realistic
* Slightly playful, not grimdark

### Art Style Keywords

Use these *consistently* in prompts:

> stylized sci-fi illustration, semi-cartoon, clean line art, soft cel shading, pleasant color palette, slightly desaturated tones, readable shapes, graphic-novel style, top-down or isometric friendly design, game-concept-art style

### World Aesthetic

* Frontier planets
* Scrappy colonies
* Modular sci-fi structures
* Mixed tech levels (hi-tech + improvised gear)
* Clean silhouettes (important for rulebook readability)

### Color Direction

* Muted earth tones + accent colors
* Dusty oranges, soft blues, sage greens
* Avoid neon overload
* Avoid ultra-dark palettes
