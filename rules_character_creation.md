# Character Creation: The Lifepath

Create your Colonist by traversing their life history.

## Step 1: Attributes
Assign the following array to your Attributes (Vigor, Dexterity, Mind, Spirit):
`+2, +1, +0, -1`

## Step 2: Childhood (Background)
Choose or roll (1d6) for your childhood.

| d6 | Background | Bonuses | Trait |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | **Urchin** | Melee +1, Social +1 | **Scrapper**: Ignore the first Bane in melee combat. |
| 2 | **Vatgrown** | Vigor +1, Shooting +1 | **Dull**: -2 to Spirit rolls, but +10 Mood. |
| 3 | **Noble Scion** | Spirit +1, Social +1 | **Greedy**: Mood penalty if bedroom is not impressive. |
| 4 | **Midworld Student** | Mind +1, Intellectual +1 | **Fast Learner**: +1 XP gain. |
| 5 | **Feral Child** | Animals +1, Plants +1 | **Wild**: Cannot do Intellectual tasks. |
| 6 | **War Refugee** | Shooting +1, Medical +1 | **Pessimist**: -5 Mood permanently. |

## Step 3: Adulthood (Terms)
You serve **Terms** of 4 years. You must take at least 1 Term, and can take up to 4.
For each Term:
1.  **Choose a Career**.
2.  **Roll Survival (2d10 + Attribute)** vs DC 12.
    *   *Success*: You survive unscathed.
    *   *Failure*: You gain a **Scar** or **Addiction**, but complete the term.
3.  **Gain Skills**: Pick 2 skills listed for the Career. (Max skill rank is 3 during creation).
4.  **Roll Event (1d6)**: See what happened to you.

### Career: Space Marine
*   **Survival**: Vigor
*   **Skills**: Shooting, Melee, Medical, Construction.
*   **Event**:
    *   1: **Disaster**: Unit wiped out. Gain **PTSD** (Trait).
    *   6: **Hero**: Promoted. Gain **Iron Will** (Trait).
    *   2-5: **Routine Patrol**: Gain +1 to one Career Skill.

### Career: Glitterworld Surgeon
*   **Survival**: Mind
*   **Skills**: Medical, Intellectual, Social, Crafting.
*   **Event**:
    *   1: **Malpractice**: Patient died. You are **Exiled**.
    *   6: **Breakthrough**: Invented a technique. Gain **Prostophile** (Trait).
    *   2-5: **Practice**: Gain +1 to Medical.

### Career: Drifter
*   **Survival**: Dexterity
*   **Skills**: Social, Melee, Plants, Construction.
*   **Event**:
    *   1: **Addiction**: Hooked on Smokeleaf or Wake-up.
    *   6: **Found Treasure**: Start with a Masterwork item.
    *   2-5: **Odd Jobs**: Gain +1 to any Skill.

## Step 4: Arrival
How did you end up on the Rimworld? (Group Decision)
1.  **Crashlanded**: Start with minimal supplies, debris scattered.
2.  **Rich Explorer**: Start with high-tech gun, turret, and silver.
3.  **Tribal**: Start with pemmican, bows, and 2 extra Colonists.
