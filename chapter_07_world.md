# Chapter 7: The Rim (World & Factions)

The Rim is a dangerous place, populated by diverse groups struggling for dominance.

---

## Factions
Your colony is not alone. You will interact with various factions, ranging from friendly traders to bloodthirsty pirates.

### Goodwill
Relationships are measured by **Goodwill**, ranging from **-100 to +100**.

| Status | Goodwill | Effects |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ally** | +75 to +100 | Will send military aid. Better trade prices (**-10% Buy / +10% Sell**). |
| **Neutral** | -9 to +74 | Will trade. Will not attack unprovoked. |
| **Hostile** | -100 to -10 | Will attack on sight. Cannot trade. |

### Major Factions
1.  **Civil Outlander Union** (Industrial): Survivors trying to rebuild. Friendly. Sources of Components/Medicine.
2.  **Rough Pig Union** (Industrial): Tough, xenophobic pig-people. Interactions are tense.
3.  **Savage Tribe** (Neolithic): Aggressive warriors. Low tech, high numbers. Source of Pemmican/Jade.
4.  **The Shattered Empire** (Spacer): Honor-bound remnants of a stellar empire. High-tech trade (Title required).
5.  **Pirate Gangs** (Industrial): **Always Hostile**. Raiders who live to plunder.

## Biomes

The environment is often your deadliest enemy.

### Temperate Forest
*   **Description**: lush forests, moderate temperatures, rich soil.
*   **Mechanics**:
    *   **Baseline**: No special penalties.
    *   **Disease**: Standard frequency.

### Arid Shrubland & Desert
*   **Description**: Hot, dry, endless sand and scrub.
*   **Mechanics**:
    *   **Heat**: Colonists without "Hot" suited apparel (Dusters, Cowboy Hats) gain **+1 Stress/Scene** during the day.
    *   **Scarce Food**: **Bane** on Foraging checks.
    *   **Movement**: Normal, but sand dunes may count as Difficult Terrain.

### Boreal Forest & Tundra
*   **Description**: Frozen pines, snow, and bitter cold.
*   **Mechanics**:
    *   **Cold**: Colonists without "Cold" suited apparel (Parkas, Tuques) take **1d4 Damage** every hour from Hypothermia.
    *   **Snow**: Deep snow counts as **Difficult Terrain** (-1 Speed, or -1 Hex/Day).
    *   **Short Growing Season**: Crops die in winter.

### Tropical Rainforest
*   **Description**: Choking vegetation, humid heat, and insects.
*   **Mechanics**:
    *   **Disease**: **Bane** on all Vigor checks to resist disease (Malaria, Sleeping Sickness).
    *   **Dense**: Movement is slow (Difficult Terrain default).
    *   **Cover**: Trees provided **Partial Cover** almost everywhere.

### Human Threats
Raiders come in many forms.

**1. Tribal Warrior** (Neolithic)
*   **Stats**: 2 / 1 / 0. **HP**: 14. **Armor**: 0 (Tribalwear).
*   **Weapon**: Ikwa (4) or Short Bow (3).
*   **Tactic**: Zerg rush. They rely on numbers.

**2. Pirate Gunner** (Industrial)
*   **Stats**: 2 / 2 / 1. **HP**: 14. **Armor**: 3 (Flak Vest).
*   **Weapon**: Autopistol (4) or Bolt-Action Rifle (6).
*   **Tactic**: Uses cover, flanks.

**3. Elite Mercenary** (Spacer)
*   **Stats**: 4 / 3 / 2. **HP**: 20. **Armor**: 6 (Marine Armor).
*   **Weapon**: Charge Rifle (8/AP2).
*   **Tactic**: coordinated, high threat.
