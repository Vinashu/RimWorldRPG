# Chapter 01 Art Prompts — Introduction & Core Mechanics

Chapter opener needed: **Yes.** This is the book’s introduction + core rules; a hero image anchors tone and identity.

---

ART NEEDED: Chapter Art
Location: Chapter 1: Introduction & Core Mechanics (opener, immediately after H1)
Purpose: establish frontier survival tone and introduce the core cast.
Placement: Chapter Opener
Prompt:
Stylized sci-fi frontier illustration in a RimWorld-like universe,
semi-cartoon style, clean line art, soft cel shading,
pleasant muted color palette, slightly desaturated,
graphic-novel / game concept art style, readable shapes.

Scene: fresh crash-landing on a rimworld; three colonists step out of smoking drop pods and survey a harsh but beautiful frontier.
Characters:
- Rurik, rugged colonist with short beard and tool belt
- Mira, medic/researcher in a light sci-fi coat holding a tablet/scanner
- Jax, shooter in patchwork armor with a rifle
Environment:
rocky plain, scattered debris, distant modular colony structures, low sun, light dust in the air.
Mood:
hopeful survival, light tension, not grimdark.
Composition:
cinematic but readable, clear silhouettes, rulebook chapter art.
Negative Prompt:
photorealistic, hyper-realistic, ultra-detailed skin,
grimdark, horror, gore,
cyberpunk neon, anime sparkle eyes,
overly dramatic lighting,
blurry, messy composition,
dark muddy colors
